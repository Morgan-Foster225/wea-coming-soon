A new path forward for women reentering their communities.

WE1A, Inc. is building a structured, accountability-based support system designed to help incarcerated and justice-involved women achieve long-term stability, emotional growth, and successful reintegration.

Our Mission

WE1A, Inc. provides structured, accountability-based support and emotional skills development designed to assist incarcerated and justice-involved women in achieving long-term stability and successful community reintegration.

Our Vision

We envision communities where reentry and recovery are supported through structured frameworks that promote accountability, opportunity, and sustainable reintegration.

Why WE1A Exists

Our purpose is to reduce barriers to successful reentry by delivering trauma-informed, accountability-based support and structured reentry services. We focus on emotional stability, workforce readiness, and long-term reintegration.


Through strategic partnerships with correctional institutions and the phased development of structured residential programming, WE1A is committed to reducing recidivism and strengthening long-term community outcomes.

Our Core Values

Dignity: Every individual deserves respect, autonomy, and the opportunity to rebuild her life without stigma.
Accountability: Structure and personal responsibility are essential for lasting change.
Empowerment: Sustainable outcomes begin with confidence, agency, and informed decision-making.
Integrity: We operate with transparency, ethical practice, and clearly defined boundaries.
Collaboration: Strong partnerships with institutions and communities create meaningful impact.
